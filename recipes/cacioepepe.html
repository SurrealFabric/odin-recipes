Cacio E Pepe Pasta

Description

Cacio e pepe is a dish with simple ingredients that proves challenging to make properly, cacio e pepe means cheese and pepper

Ingredients

  • 200g of Spaghetti
  • 1 cube of butter
  • 10g of salt
  • Grated pecorino cheese, you must grate the cheese yourself
  • Black Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a rolling boil
  2. Add 10g of salt to the water
  3. Once the pasta has cooked, strain it, but save some pasta water
  4. Place the pasta back in the empty pot and add the butter and pasta water
  5. Mix well, the finally start adding pecorino cheese while mixing
  6. Once the sauce is properly emulsified, add pepper and serve
