Amatriciana Pasta

Description

Amatriciana pasta derives it's name from Amatrice, a town in Abruzzo where this pasta originates from

Ingredients

  • 200g Bucatini Pasta
  • 200g Peeled Tomatoes
  • 10g Salt
  • 100g Guanciale
  • 1 Whole Chilli Pepper
  • 1/2 Glass Of Cooking Wine
  • Pecorino Cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a rolling boil
  2. Add salt to the water and proceed to add the pasta
  3. In a pan, heat up the guanciale until the fat is transparent
  4. Break apart the peeled tomatoes and add them to the pan with guanciale
  5. Lower the heat and let the sauce simmer, adding a dash of wine and chilli pepper
  6. Once the sauce is sufficiently reduced, strain the pasta and mix it with the sauce
  7. Sprinkle with pecorino cheese to taste>
