Carbonara Pasta Recipe

Description

Carbonara is a traditional Italian dish, it derives from a nutritious yet simple dish that was cooked for coal mine workers, from the Italian carbone, meaning coal.

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 3 eggs
  • 100g Guanciale
  • 30g Pecorino Romano
  • 10g Table Salt
  • Black Pepper to taste

    • Steps

    1. Bring a pot of water to a rolling boil
    2. Add 10g of salt
    3. Cut guanciale into thin strips
    4. Put the guanciale stripes into a heated pan, cook until fat turns transparent
    5. Pour 1 egg yolk and 1 full egg into a bowl, agitate them well
    6. add pepper and pecorino to the bowl, amalgamate with a wooden spoon
    7. Once the pasta is cooked al dente, strain it and add it into the bowl
    8. Place the guanciale with the fat into the bowl and mix well
    9. Sprinkle pecorino cheese to taste
